    WHO AM I?

    I am a web developer and an avid film watcher. I am a book worm and a coffee drinker. Sometimes I like to say that I scared communism right out of Romania, because I was born just a couple of months before it fell, in a quiet Romanian town.

    That town was so quiet that I soon got bored and was on my way to the big city, not really knowing what will become of me and so I embarked on a series of adventures, gathering experience in a variety of fields.

    Soon, Bucharest wasn’t enough for me and I decided to travel north to see how others lived and laughed. I stopped in a place they call the happiest country in the world.

    Copenhagen - it was here that I discovered what I would now call the job of a lifetime! Ok, I’m making it sound a little bit too glamorous, but it’s only to convey how deeply I am invested in my work.
